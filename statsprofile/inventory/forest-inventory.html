Forest inventory

Forest inventory in Canada consists of ongoing data collection relating in particular to forest composition and use. This inventory provides the data necessary to inform political decisions, formulate regulations related to forestry, conduct scientific research, prepare management plans and monitor sustainability of forest functions over time. This data collection is collaborative involving federal, provincial and territorial governments.
