Revenue from goods manufactured

Trends in revenue from goods manufactured in the forestry and logging, pulp and paper product manufacturing, and wood product manufacturing sub-sectors. Revenue from goods manufactured includes revenue from the sale of goods manufactured using materials owned by the establishment, as well as from repair work, manufacturing service charges and work contracted to others.
